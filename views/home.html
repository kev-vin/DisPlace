DisPlace - a Hack Week Project

This week, hundreds of brilliant people are creating super cool projects, from clever & wacky Discord bots to sweet artwork, music, and stories. For our submission, we really wanted to challenge ourselves by teaching ourselves more about awesome open source technologies and testing the scope of Discord's powerful developer API. We had tonnes of fun on this project, and are excited to play around with the other great submissions! Displace is a collaborative drawing tool, where individual communities are encouraged to organise themselves and work together to create art on a shared canvas across all connected communities. We're excited to see what you will make! Add your community to join the fun.
